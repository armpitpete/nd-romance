# ND Readable Romance Mini Design Sheet v1.0

## Purpose

Use this file when the full Design Sheet feels too large.

This is the first-pass version only.
It is designed to get a romance idea moving without overloading the start.

After this, move to the full Design Sheet or the Structure Template.

---

## Good enough rule

Do not perfect the idea here.

Good enough means you can answer:

- who are the two people?
- why these two?
- what makes love difficult?
- what ND-readable truth matters?
- what must the ending prove?

Stop when you have:

- one simple romance definition
- one real obstacle
- one ND-readable truth

- one first scene seed
