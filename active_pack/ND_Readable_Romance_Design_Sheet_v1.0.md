# ND Readable Romance Design Sheet v1.0

## Core use

This file is the **entry-point design file** for the ND-readable romance pack.

Use it before:
- chapter planning
- beat mapping
- scene drafting
- revision planning

Its job is to stop:
- vague pair design
- generic attraction
- fake conflict chosen too late
- “good premise, weak relationship” syndrome
- drafting before the emotional engine exists

This file defines:
- who the romance is about
- why these two specifically
- what kind of romance it is
- what the emotional promise is
- what makes it ND-readable
- what makes love difficult
- what must change for the ending to work

---

# Core rule

A romance is not properly designed until you can answer:

- Why these two?
- Why now?
- Why is love difficult here?
- What kind of care becomes possible between them?
- What must change before union is believable?

If those are unclear, the manuscript is not ready for structure.
